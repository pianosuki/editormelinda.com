Welcome to Edit Soap. I make small batches of high-quality soap using simple ingredients: goat milk, tallow, coconut oil, and olive oil. To create variety, I mix things up with essential oils, clays, botanicals, phthalate-free fragrances, and natural colorants (mineral oxides and mica). What makes Edit Soap special is the use of 9% butterfat milk from our Nigerian Dwarf dairy goats. Goat milk contains many compounds that are so good for your skin, such as alpha hydroxyl acids, essential fatty acids, triglycerides, vitamins A, B, C, D, and E, antioxidants, and selenium.

Edit Soap can be found at Away with Words bookshop on Front Street in Poulsbo. I also sell my soap through this website for $6/bar. (The names of the soaps are based on the fragrance used; check BrambleBerry for descriptions of the scents.) Please email me at editormelinda@outlook.com to order. You can pick up at the farm, or I can deliver via my cargo bike for $5 within the City of Poulsbo.
